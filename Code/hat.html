Chunky Hat

Materials Needed

  • Bulky yarn, wool is best (size 5)

  • Size K Hook (6.5mm)

  • Yarn needle

  • Scissors

    • Stitch Tutorials

  • Chain Stitch (CH)

  • Slip Stitch (SS)

  • Single Crochet (SC)

  • Half Double Crochet (HDC)

  • Back Loop Only (BLO)

    • Pattern

  • Note: This pattern is for a 20in circumference hat. You can add or subtract rows to make it larger or smaller to fit your head.

  • Beginning: CH 36

  • Row 1: HDC in 3rd CH from hook, HDC in next 30 stitches then SC in remaining 5 stitches. Turn (35)

  • Row 2: CH 1, 5 SC BLO, 30 HDC BLO, turn (35)

  • Row 3: CH 1, 30 HDC BLO, 5 SC BLO, turn (35)

  • Repeat rows 2 and 3 until it measures 19.5in on the long side.

  • Finishing: Fold the work in half and SS both sides together. Cut yarn leaving a long tail. Thread a yarn needle with the tail and weave in and out around the stitches at the top of the hat then pull it closed. Knot the yarn to secure it then weave in all ends.

  • You can also add a pom pom or furry pom to the top of the hat!
