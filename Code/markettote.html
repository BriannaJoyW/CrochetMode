Market Tote Bag

Materials Needed

  • Cotton Yarn (You can use more than one color if you would like!)

  • Size H Hook (5mm)

  • Scissors

  • Stitch Marker

  • Yarn Needle

    • Stitch Tutorials

  • Chain Stitch (CH)

  • Single Crochet (SC)

  • Single Crochet 2 Together (SC2TOG)

    • Pattern

  • Do not join to beginning of previous rows as you work, place a stitch marker when you begin a new row instead.

  • Beginning: Make a magic loop

  • Row 1: CH 1, work 8 SC into the magic loop then pull loop tightly closed.

  • Row 2: 2 SC in each stitch around.
  • Row 3: *1 SC in the next stitch, 2 SC in the next stitch*, repeat from * to * around.
  • Row 4: *1 SC in the next 2 stitches, 2 SC in the next stitch*, repeat from * to * around.
  • Row 5: *1 SC in the next 3 stitches, 2 SC in the next stitch*, repeat from * to * around.
  • Row 6: *1 SC in the next 4 stitches, 2 SC in the next stitch*, repeat from * to * around.
  • Row 7: *1 SC in the next 5 stitches, 2 SC in the next stitch*, repeat from * to * around.
  • Rows 8-15: SC in each stitch around.
  • Row 16: *1 SC in the next 5 stitches, SC2TOG*, repeat from * to * around.
  • Row 17: SC in each stitch around.
  • Row 18: *1 SC in the next 4 stitches, SC2TOG*, repeat from * to * around.
  • Row 19-25: SC in each stitch around.
  • Row 26: SC in the next 7 stitches, CH 45, skip next 6 stitches, SC in the next 14 stitches, CH 45, skip next 6 stitches, SC in the next 7 stitches.
  • Row 27: SC in the next 7 stitches, 45 SC (across the CH 45 from previous row), SC in the next 14 stitches, 45 SC (across the CH 45 from previous row), SC in the next 7 stitches.
  • Row 28: SC in each stitch around.
  • Finishing: Tie off and weave all ends in.
