Ear Warmer Headband

Materials Needed

  • Bulky or acrylic yarn (size 4 or 5)

  • Size K Hook (6.5mm)

  • Yarn needle

  • Scissors

    • Stitch Tutorials

  • Chain Stitch (CH)

  • Slip Stitch (SS)

  • Single Crochet (SC)

  • Half Double Crochet (HDC)

  • Back Loop Only (BLO)

    • Pattern

  • Note: This pattern is for a 20in circumference headband. You can add or subtract rows to make it larger or smaller to fit your head.

  • Beginning: CH 12

  • Row 1: Starting in 3rd CH from hook, HDC across CH row. Turn.

  • Row 2: CH 2, HDC BLO across. Turn.

  • Repeat row 2 until the work measures 19.5in.

  • Finishing: Fold the work in half and SS both sides together. Cut yarn leaving a tail. Knot the yarn to secure it then thred the yarn tail into the yarn needle and weave in all ends.
