Classic Granny Square

Materials Needed

  • You can use any yarn you would like (size 4)

  • Size H Hook (5mm)

  • Scissors

    • Stitch Tutorials

  • Chain Stitch (CH)

  • Single Crochet (SC)

  • Double Crochet (DC)

  • Slip Stitch (SS)

    • Pattern

  • Beginning: Make a magic circle

  • Row 1: CH 3, 2 DC into the magic circle, *CH 3, 3 DC into magic circle*, repeat * to * until you have 4 sets of DC and 4 sets of CH's (this will look like a square), join with a SS to the 3rd CH of the beginning CH stitches.

  • Row 2: CH 4 (this counts as 1 DC and 1 CH), *in the next CH 3 space work 3 DC, CH 3, 3 DC, CH 1*, repeat * to * around until the last CH 3 space where you will work 3 DC, CH 3, 2 DC, then join with a SS to the 3rd CH from the beginning CH stitches.

  • Row 3: CH 3 (this counts as 1 DC), work 2 DC into the CH 3 space, CH 1, *in the next CH 3 space work 3 DC, CH 3, 3 DC, CH 1*, repeat * to * then join with a SS to the 3rd CH from the beginning CH stitches.

  • Size: You can make this as large as you would like by repeating rows 2 and 3!
