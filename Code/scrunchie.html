Hair Scrunchie

Materials Needed

  • Yarn of your choice, any texture you like (size 4)

  • Size G Hook (4mm)

  • Scissors

  • Hair Elastic

  • Yarn Needle

  • Safety Pin

    • Stitch Tutorials

  • Chain Stitch (CH)

  • Slip Stitch (SS)

  • Half Double Crochet (HDC)

    • Pattern

  • Beginning: CH 17

  • Row 1: HDC into 3rd CH from hook, HDC in each stitch across.

  • Row 2: CH 2 (count as 1st stitch), HDC into 2nd stitch in the row, HDC in each stitch across.

  • Rows 3-50: Repeat row 2. Fasten off leaving a long tail.

  • Finishing: Take the elastic band and place the safety pin on one end. Scrunch up your scrunchie, and pop your elastic band through it; once this is done, tie both sides of the elastic band together.
