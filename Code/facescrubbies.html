Reusable Face Scrubbies

Materials Needed

  • Cotton Yarn (color of your choice)

  • Size G Hook (4mm)

  • Yarn needle

  • Scissors

    • Stitch Tutorials

  • Magic Circle (MC)

  • Chain Stitch (CH)

  • Slip Stitch (SS)

  • Single Crochet (SC)

  • Puff Stitch (PS)

    • Pattern

  • Row 1: MC, 11 SC into the center of the MC, SS into your first SC. (11)

  • Row 2: CH 1, PS into the same stitch then into each SC stitch around. PS into the SS from round 1, this makes a total of 12 PS in this row. (12)

  • Row 3: CH 1, *SC into the next space inbetween two PS, SC in PS*, repeat * to * around then SS into the first SC. (23)

  • Row 4: CH 1, PS into the same stitch then into each SC stitch around. Ps into the SS from round 3, this makes a total of 24 PS in this row. (24)

  • Row 5: CH 1, *SC into the next space inbetween two PS, SC in PS*, repeat * to * around then SS into the first SC. (47)

  • Finish: Cut yarn with a tail, then pull yarn through the stitch that is on your hook. Thread yarn tail through yarn needle and weave the tail through the SC stitches.
